ABOUT CUISINES

Nestled in the luxury premises of Hilton, the best hotels in Sri Lanka, Curry leaf restaurant is the best dining spot to book your table. Know what makes the curry leaf, one of the best restaurants in Colombo is its exclusive menu and the cozy settings. The luxuriating premises of Hilton compliments the causal yet fascinating ambiance of the eatery. The wooden seating, earthy tones, and illuminating lights have enhanced every corner of the restaurant. The menu of the curry leaf menu offers the best traditional taste, and some flavors inspired by the far-off places. Do try the Chili Crab, a featured dish that is served as top-selling on the menu. Further elaborating the menu, the curry leaf has an irresistible seafood buffet that cannot be missed. Live Music and the band are also organized every evening to entertain the guests. The Curry leaf is designed with a village-themed setup and offers a cool and soothing ambiance for a peaceful dinner. Location: 2, SIR CHITTAMPALAM A GARDINER MW, Lotus Rd, Colombo

Best From the Menu: Kottu roti, fish, cuttlefish, prawns and crabs, local sweetmeats, curd with treacle.
